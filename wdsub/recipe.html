Post Apocalyptic Cake

  1. Requirments:

    • Ingredients:

      • Flour
      • Sugar
      • Salt
      • Eggs
      • Butter
      • Enriched Uranium

    • Materials:

      • 2 Bowls
      • Mixer
      • Oven
      • Cake pan
      • Mortar and pestal

  2. Batter

    1. Preheat Oven to 4000 derees farenheit
    2. Add Flour to bowl
    3. Add butter
    4. Add two eggs to bowl
    5. Add butter
    6. Add two tablespoons of sugar
    7. Add butter
    8. Add one tablespoon of salt
    9. Add butter
    10. Add butter
    11. Add butter
    12. Add butter
    13. Mix on high for one minute
    14. Add butter
    15. Mix on high for 14 minutes
    16. Add to cake pan
    17. bake for 24 Hours

  3. Frosting

    1. Add sugar to bowl
    2. Add butter
    3. Crush uranium with mortar and pestal and add
    4. Add butter
    5. Mix
    6. Cool for 5 days (should be glowing green when done)

  4. Preperation

    1. Grab cake
    2. Spread frosting all over
    3. Crush up more uranium and sprinkle on top
    4. Enjoy.
